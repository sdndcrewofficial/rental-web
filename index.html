Questions & Suggestions (FAQ)

How do I book a car?

You can easily book a car by using the "Book Your Car" form on our homepage. Simply select your desired pickup and return locations, dates, and times, then click "Search Cars" to find available vehicles.

What are the requirements to rent a car?

Generally, you need to be at least 21 years old (some car categories may require 25+), possess a valid driver's license for at least one year, and have a valid credit card in your name for the security deposit.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation online through your account or by contacting our customer service. Please refer to our Terms of Service for specific cancellation policies and fees.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Absolutely! We provide competitive rates for long-term rentals (monthly or longer). Please contact us directly for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

What if I have a suggestion for improvement?

We value your feedback! Please feel free to use the contact information in our footer to send us your suggestions. We're always looking for ways to enhance your experience.
